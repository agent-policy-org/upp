# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the UPP community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community and protocol users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Disruptive, hostile, or exclusionary behavior
- Sustained off-topic arguing or bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take fair, appropriate corrective action in response to violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security/administration issue or contact maintainers through repository channels. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may use the following response scale:

1. Correction: Private or public warning for minor conduct issues
2. Warning: Temporary restrictions for repeated or serious issues
3. Temporary Ban: Time-bound exclusion from interactions
4. Permanent Ban: Long-term exclusion for severe or repeated abuse
